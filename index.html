Pathways- Group of Schools

The Pathways Schools are non-public special education schools that provide diverse quality programs to meet the individual needs of students ages 11-21 with emotional and behavioral disabilities. ... Pathways is committed to the development and dissemination of professional best practices in the field of special education.

  • Changes in the school because of Pandemic

    This year, the pandemic cast a dark shadow on the world of cinema, with theatres being shut indefinitely. However, the crisis gave time to people to reflect and engage in producing art that could be channelised through a digital medium for the world to see. Resultantly, Changes, a unique project, turned into a short film that saw the participation of many people who were entering a film set for the first time, but had the passion and zeal to create a powerful piece of art. Changes was a concept that germinated from the reality of times around us. Primarily, it was only something that would be shot on the phone in a closed apartment in Mumbai, but circumstances allowed us to experiment and use DSLR cameras to shoot the film. Even then, the film had a very limited number of people, with a relatively inexperienced cast and crew. With a shoestring budget, the team aimed to create something visually powerful and allow people to connect with something that affected the entire world.
